Why Copenhagen?

Copenhagen, the city of fairy tales, bicycles, and hygge

According to legend, Copenhagen was founded by the Viking warrior Bishop Absalon in 1167, who built a fortress on the site where the city now stands. The city’s rich history is filled with tales of royalty, seafaring, and fairy tales inspired by Hans Christian Andersen.

Copenhagen is renowned for its vibrant culture, innovative cuisine, and sustainable lifestyle. The city is famous for its colorful Nyhavn harbor, historic castles, and world-class museums. Whether you're exploring the charming streets by bike, enjoying a coffee in a cozy café, or visiting the iconic Little Mermaid statue, Copenhagen offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Its commitment to green living and friendly atmosphere make it a must-visit destination for travelers from around the world.
